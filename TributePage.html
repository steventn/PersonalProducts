Masashi Kishimoto

Biography

  • November 1974: Born in Okayama, Japan
  • 1996: Honored with monthly 'Hop Step Award' after submitting pilot of manga 'Karakuri'
  • September 1996: Serialized version of 'Naruto' was published
  • November 2014: Over 200 million copies of 'Naruto' was sold by the time it ended
  • December 2015: Annouced supervision of 'Bortuo' series
  • 2019: Announced new science fiction adventure series called 'Samurai 8: The Tale of Hachimaru'
